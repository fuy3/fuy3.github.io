Zhuang ethnic minority is the largest minority group om China. Over 90% of the Zhuang people live in Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region.
Clothing
The main colors for Zhuang's clothing are blue, black and brown. The types of clothing are different in different areas, especially for the women. In the northwest Guangxi, the women wear collarless, embroidered jackets buttoned yo the left along with loose and wide trousers, or pleated shirts and embroidered belts. In the southeast Guangxi, they wear collarless jackets buttoned to the left along with black square headbands on the head and loose trousers. Both women and men wear cloth shoes. The Zhuang women are skilled at weaving and embroidering.
Festivals
The Zhuang people not only celebrate the traditional festivals like Chinese Spring Festival, Dragon Boat Festival, Mid-Autumn Festival, Double Ninth Festival, but also celebrated some their own festivals such as Zhuang New Year, Singing Festival, Ox Soul Festival, Liuyueliu Festival, Qiyueqi Festival, Luotuo Festival, Yabai Festival, Chili Festival, Longduan Festival. Among the festivals, Singing Festival and Zhuang New Year are the well-known ones.
