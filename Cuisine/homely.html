Homely Meal

Homely meal are dishes that are prepared for daily consumption at home. Home cooking, which is the source of Chinese cuisine, is also the basis for the composition of local flavor dishes. Dishes that can also be stir-fried by families using available condiments. In most Chinese families, especially in the southern China, rice, meat and vegetables dishes are combined to make a standard family meal.

Rice


Rice

Rice is a staple food loved by people in China, and even in East and Southeast Asia, and is made by steaming or simmering rice and the right amount of water. Rice is a food made by boiling rice with water. Rice is the most staple food in East Asia, including China, Japan and Korea. Rice is prepared by adding one cup of rice to one and a half to one cup of water. The rice is cooked to swell appropriately and is fully cooked inside, and the water is removed.
The introduction of rice can be traced back to congee. Since the water requirement for cooking congee is lower than that of rice, the ancient Chinese people gradually mastered the effect of cooking with different amounts of water and developed a fuller rice. In modern times, as the technology of polishing grains has become more advanced than in ancient times, the rice consumed by the people has gradually changed from brown rice to white rice.

Meet

The meat dishes in Chinese food are mainly chicken, duck, fish, pork, beef, eggs, milk and other foods. Almost all of these dishes can be fried, stir-fried, cooked, stewed or braised. The followings are a few famous homely meat dishes:


Fried pork with chili

fried pork with chili

Stir-fried pork with chili peppers is a classic home-cooked dish in China Hunan cuisine, requiring only simple chili peppers and five-flower meat to become the leading dish in Hunan cuisine. The unique taste of these two ingredients after the collision is unbeknownst to the people of Hunan and even the world, a dish that every family will make.

Stewed chicken with mushroom

The origin of chicken and mushroom stew is also very much a part of life in Northeast China. It is a stew made with dried mushrooms, chicken and vermicelli. The best mushrooms for chicken stew are wild hazel mushrooms, the kind with a thin pole and thin umbrella, which can maximize the freshness of the chicken meat. A veritable mountain treasure and wild food. One of the few Northeastern dishes that can be developed into a home-style dish comparable to other high-end dishes.

Vegetables


Vegetables in market

Chinese vegetables are dishes made from plant and mushroom foods. Chinese vegetarian cuisine has a long history, it was born in the Spring and Autumn and Warring States period, mainly used for rituals and major ceremonies. In the Wei and Jin Dynasties and the North and South Dynasties, with the introduction of Buddhism, the theory of "vegetarianism" was gradually formed, which played a great role in promoting the development of vegetarian cuisine. Since then, vegetarian cuisine has become its own system, unique and distinctive, and has become an important part of the colorful Chinese cuisine and food culture.
