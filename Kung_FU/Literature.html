Martial arts novel

Martial arts novel, is the popular literature in Chinese literature, is a kind of novel type that combines history and fantasy in the Chinese region after the Republic of China, and the retrospective origin can be traced to the the Tang Dynasty, the story mainly describes the feud between wulin gangs, the feud between people in rivers and lakes, and the chivalrous spirit of the nation-state. The historical background of martial arts novels is mostly ancient China, especially the change of dynasties, the chaos of the Han and foreign wars, so the current situation creates heroes; In the midst of the Gaein chaos, there is a greater need for people to prove their worth by force. With chivalrousity as the theme and martial arts as the means, martial arts novels are unique cultural characteristics, and take ancient Chinese medicine, Buddhist studies, Taoists, easy art and various mystics. Therefore, the values ​​of the characters in the martial arts novels, and even the spir itual tone of the whole work, are based on the basic idea of ​​the hero's righteousness. Martial arts novels are popular in Chinese literary circles at home and abroad, and readers are still enthusiastic. In addition, due to the successive popularization of television and computers, martial arts novels have surpassed the previous use of text symbols as a means of expression. They have also used multiple and multiple symbol forms, combined with sounds, color pictures, color pictures, and actions, to set off a wave in society.
